Travel Bucket

Some places to visit in Kerala

Munnar

Famous for its breathtaking tea gardens and cool, misty weather. Visit Top Station for panoramic views of the Western Ghats and explore tea factories

Cochin

Explore the historic Jew Town with its ancient synagogues and antique shops, or witness a traditional Kathakali dance performance at a theatre.

Wayanad

This hilly region offers trekking opportunities to places like Chembara Peak and provides a mix of history, culture, and natural beauty.

Alappuzha

This is quintessential experience. Cruise the serene backwaters on a houseboat, enjoying traditional meals and the tranquility of village life.
